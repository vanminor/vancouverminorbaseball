Season Structure & Locations

Our PeeWee, Bantam, Midget teams play home games at Nanaimo Park, with away games across other local association parks.

In the spring season, the Pee Wee, Bantam, and Midget divisions participate in tiered competition across A, AA, and AAA levels, ensuring players are challenged at the appropriate level and can grow in a competitive, supportive setting.
